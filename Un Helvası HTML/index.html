Flour Halva

Halva, which comes from the steppe culture, is a dessert of mourning and happiness for the Turks. It was held in palaces on special and important occasions. Today, it is one of the indispensable desserts of Turkish cuisine.


Recipe: M.Fatih BATUR

Flour Halva Ingredients

Halva

  • 2 cups flour

  • 1 glass of oil

Sherbet

  • 2 and half glassess of water

  • 1 and half cups of granulated sugar

How to make Halva?

  1. First, let's prepare the sherbet.

  2. Let's put granulated sugar and water in a pot and let it melt over low heat.

  3. Let's put oil and flour in a large steel pot.

  4. Let's roast it over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the smell of the flour disappears.

  5. When the smell of flour disappears, let's turn the heat down.

  6. Let it roast until the color of the flour darkens.

  7. When it reaches the desired color, pour the warm sherbet you took from the stove over it.

  8. There may be splashes when pouring, be careful.

  9. After pouring the sherbet, let's stir it quickly to absorb it.

  10. Let's mix it by pressing it well with the back of the spoon.

  11. After your halva is cooled, put it in a spoon, turn it upside down and place it on a serving plate.

  12. After it has cooled, you can sprinkle cinnamon or coconut as desired.
